Website privacy

Privacy Policy

Magnolia Senior Care respects the trust families place in us. This page explains how information submitted through this website may be collected, used, and protected.

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

In plain language

Magnolia does not sell personal information. Website contact submissions are used to respond to family questions, tour requests, referrals, and care inquiries.

Contact Magnolia

Information we may collect

When you use this website, Magnolia may receive information you choose to share through contact forms, tour request forms, referral inquiries, email links, or phone links. This may include your name, phone number, email address, preferred location, care questions, timing, and information you provide about a family care situation.

The website may also collect general usage information, such as browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location information, referring pages, and basic analytics data.

How information is used

Magnolia uses website information to respond to inquiries, schedule tours, communicate with families or referral partners, understand website performance, improve educational resources, and maintain the security and reliability of the website.

Magnolia does not sell personal information.

Educational website content and health information

Magnolia's Resource Center articles are educational and separate from HIPAA-protected medical records. Submitting a website form does not create a medical record, provider-patient relationship, or emergency communication channel.

If you share sensitive information through a form, Magnolia will use reasonable care in handling it, but families should avoid submitting urgent medical details through the website. For emergencies, call 911 or local emergency services.

Analytics, cookies, and website tools

This website may use cookies, analytics, maps, embedded media, form tools, hosting services, or other third-party website services. These tools may collect technical or usage information according to their own privacy practices.

Safeguards

Magnolia uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information submitted through the website. No website or email system can guarantee complete security, so families should use care when sharing sensitive information online.

External links

This website may link to external websites such as maps, social media, video platforms, government resources, or professional resources. Magnolia is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for adults seeking senior care information. Magnolia does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to Magnolia Senior Care through the contact information on this website.

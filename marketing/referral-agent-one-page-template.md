## Best-Fit Referral Context

- Family wants a private home setting with familiar routines
- Resident needs personal care, medication reminders, meals, hydration, and daily support
- Resident may benefit from memory-aware routines, gentle cueing, and a calm environment
- POA or family needs responsive communication during a sensitive transition
- Referral partner needs quick location fit and tour scheduling

## Referral Conversation Checklist

- Preferred location: Burien or Des Moines
- Resident summary and current care setting
- Mobility, transfer, fall-risk, cognition, medication, diet, and behavioral considerations
- Family or POA contact information
- Timeline and urgency
- Relevant discharge, assessment, or care plan documents

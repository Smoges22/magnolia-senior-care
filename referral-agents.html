What helps us respond quickly

To help us respond quickly, include:

  • Preferred location: Burien, Des Moines, or either
  • Care needs: mobility, memory, medications, supervision
  • Timeline: urgent discharge, near-term move, or planning ahead
  • Contact: family, POA, case manager, or referring professional

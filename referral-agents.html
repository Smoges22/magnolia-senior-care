Referral process

Simple Adult Family Home referral support for care teams and families.

  • Case managers, discharge planners, and referral agents can call or email with preferred city, resident summary, and timeline.
  • Talk through mobility, memory, medication routines, diet, transfer needs, and family priorities.
  • Confirm whether Burien or Des Moines is the best location fit.
  • Schedule a family tour or professional walkthrough.
  • Review documents needed for assessment and move-in planning.

For referral partners

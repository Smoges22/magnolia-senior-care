Referral support

How Magnolia supports referrals

Magnolia helps case managers, discharge planners, referral agents, and hospital teams understand whether Burien or Des Moines may be the right next step.

1. Quick response

Prompt placement support

We respond promptly so families and care teams are not left waiting during discharge or placement decisions.

2. RN-led care decisions

Clinical care-fit guidance

Care fit conversations are guided by a registered nurse with real clinical experience.

3. Clear communication

Straightforward updates

You receive straightforward updates on availability, needs, and next steps.

4. Reliable follow-through

Move-in planning support

We keep families and referral partners informed from first contact through move-in planning.

What helps us respond quickly

Send the basics, and we will guide the next step.

  • Location preference: Burien, Des Moines, or either Magnolia home
  • Care needs: mobility, memory, medication routines, transfers, diet, and supervision
  • Timeline: urgent discharge, near-term move, or planning ahead
  • Best family, POA, case manager, or referral contact

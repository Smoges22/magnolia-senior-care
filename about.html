Meet Magnolia

RN-led Adult Family Home care with a personal touch families can feel.

Magnolia Senior Care is guided by a simple idea: care should feel personal, calm, and consistent — not institutional.

Each Magnolia home supports up to six residents in a real home environment, where routines are familiar, communication is direct, and every person is known beyond their care needs.

Founder-led care

Sosena Mekuria, RN Provider

Magnolia was built by a registered nurse who has spent over 10 years supporting seniors and families through some of life’s most important transitions.

That experience shapes how care is delivered every day — through careful observation, steady routines, and a calm, respectful environment.

At Magnolia, residents are not just cared for.

They are understood.

Their routines, preferences, personalities, and family relationships all matter here.

Why families choose Magnolia

Our approach

Care that feels human, not institutional

Families often come to Magnolia during uncertain moments.

We focus on making things clear, calm, and manageable.

Instead of navigating a large system, families work directly with a small team who understands the household, the routines, and the care plan.

Care is not rushed or generalized.

It is shaped intentionally around each resident’s life.

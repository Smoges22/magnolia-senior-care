Meet Magnolia

RN-Owned Adult Family Home care with a personal touch families can feel.

Magnolia Senior Care is led by Sosena Mekuria, RN Provider. Her nursing background helps shape a calm, attentive Adult Family Home experience for up to 6 residents in each home.

Founder-led care

Sosena Mekuria, RN Provider

Magnolia began with a registered nurse who has spent more than a decade caring for seniors and supporting families through important transitions. That experience shows up in the way each home is run: with careful observation, dignity, steady routines, and a calm environment.

At Magnolia, residents are known as whole people. Their stories, routines, preferences, and family relationships matter here.

  • RN-owned and RN-managed care perspective
  • Personalized plans for physical, emotional, and social needs
  • Warm, direct communication with families, POAs, and care partners
  • Two local homes serving Burien, Des Moines, Highline, and South King County

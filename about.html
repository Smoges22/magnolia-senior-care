Meet Magnolia

RN-led residential care with a personal touch families can feel.

Magnolia Senior Care is guided by a simple idea: care should feel personal, calm, and consistent — not institutional.

Each Magnolia home supports up to six residents in a real home environment, where routines are familiar, communication is direct, and every person is known beyond their care needs.

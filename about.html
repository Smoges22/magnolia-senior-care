Meet Magnolia

RN-owned care with a deeply personal reason for showing up well.

Magnolia Senior Care is led by Sosena Mekuria, RN Provider, whose nursing background shapes a warmer, more attentive Adult Family Home experience for up to 6 residents in each home.

Founder-led care

Sosena Mekuria, RN Provider

Magnolia's story begins with a registered nurse who has spent more than a decade caring for seniors and families. That experience is the center of the brand: careful observation, individualized care planning, dignity, and a calm home environment.

At Magnolia, aging is treated as a meaningful life chapter. Residents are known as whole people, with personal histories, routines, preferences, and family relationships that deserve respect.

  • RN-owned and RN-managed care perspective
  • Personalized plans for physical, emotional, and social needs
  • Warm, direct communication with families, POAs, and care partners
  • Two local homes serving Burien, Des Moines, Highline, and South King County

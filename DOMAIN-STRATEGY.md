# Magnolia Senior Care Domain Strategy

## Recommendation

Use one umbrella brand domain for the full Magnolia Adult Family Home website.

Final recommended domain direction:

`magnoliaseniorcarewa.com`, if available.

Optional defensive redirect domain:

`magnoliaadultfamilyhomes.com`, if available.

Do not use `magnoliaseniorcare.com`; it is already an active Magnolia Senior Care nursing home website in Mississippi.

## Competitive Domain Landscape

Two nearby domain names create possible confusion:

- `magnoliacarehomes.com` is already used by a Magnolia Assisted Living and Memory Care brand with communities in multiple states, including Texas, Maine, and Pennsylvania.
- `magnoliacare.com` currently redirects to a premium healthcare domain listing for sale.
- `magnoliaseniorcare.com` is already used by a Magnolia Senior Care nursing home in Jackson, Mississippi.

Because of that, Magnolia should not position itself as a generic "Magnolia care homes" brand. The website should clearly signal:

- Washington-based Adult Family Homes.
- Burien and Des Moines locations.
- RN-owned and RN-managed care.
- Small residential care for up to 6 residents.
- Direct family communication.
- Real home environment, not a large assisted living campus.

## Why One Umbrella Domain

Magnolia currently has two separate location websites:

- `magnoliaseniorlovingcare.com`
- `magnoliaseniorcareofhighline.com`

Those names are useful for location identity, but they split SEO authority, confuse referrals, and make the brand feel less polished. A single Magnolia brand domain is stronger because it:

- Builds one trusted brand.
- Consolidates backlinks and search authority.
- Gives families one place to compare both homes.
- Makes referral-agent outreach simpler.
- Looks more professional than separate Google Sites.
- Supports future locations without another rebuild.

## Recommended URL Structure

Use short, clear, location-specific URLs:

- Homepage: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/`
- Burien location: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/burien-adult-family-home.html`
- Des Moines location: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/des-moines-adult-family-home.html`
- Services: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/services.html`
- Gallery: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/rooms-photos.html`
- Referral agents: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/referral-agents.html`
- Contact: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/contact.html`
- Blog: `https://magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/blog/afh-education-template.html`

## Redirect Plan For Existing Websites

Keep the old domains and redirect them to the new umbrella site.

Recommended redirects:

- `magnoliaseniorlovingcare.com/home` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/burien-adult-family-home.html`
- `magnoliaseniorlovingcare.com/about` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/about.html`
- `magnoliaseniorlovingcare.com/gallery` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/rooms-photos.html`
- `magnoliaseniorlovingcare.com/contact` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/contact.html`
- `magnoliaseniorcareofhighline.com/home` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/des-moines-adult-family-home.html`
- `magnoliaseniorcareofhighline.com/about` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/about.html`
- `magnoliaseniorcareofhighline.com/gallery` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/rooms-photos.html`
- `magnoliaseniorcareofhighline.com/contact` -> `magnoliaseniorcarewa.com/contact.html`

Use permanent `301` redirects where hosting allows it.

## SEO Risks To Avoid

- Do not publish the same copy on both location pages.
- Do not leave old Google Sites pages live without redirects.
- Do not use multiple domains with identical content.
- Do not point both Google Business Profiles only to the homepage.
- Do not use fake addresses, alternate phone numbers, or inconsistent NAP details.
- Do not keyword-stuff city names.
- Do not call the homes generic "senior living facilities" when the search intent is Adult Family Home care.
- Do not use photo-heavy pages without compressed images.
- Do not launch without a sitemap and Search Console submission.
- Do not change URLs repeatedly after launch.

## Google Business Profile URL Strategy

Use location-specific URLs:

- Magnolia Senior Loving Care LLC: `/burien-adult-family-home.html`
- Magnolia Senior Care of Highline LLC: `/des-moines-adult-family-home.html`

This improves local relevance and helps families land on the page that matches the Google listing they clicked.

## Final Domain Recommendation

Choose:

`magnoliaseniorcarewa.com`

Reason:

It is short, brand-wide, and clearly different from large assisted living, memory care, and nursing home brands. It makes the Adult Family Home model the point of difference.

Also buy `magnoliaadultfamilyhomes.com` if available and redirect it to the same website. This longer domain is less elegant, but it is very clear for families who do not know what "AFH" means yet.

## How Magnolia Differentiates From Similar Domains

### Avoid broad "care homes" positioning

`magnoliacarehomes.com` already owns the broader assisted living / memory care association. `magnoliaseniorcare.com` is also already used by a nursing home. Magnolia should avoid competing directly on broad senior-care language.

Use more precise language instead:

- "Adult Family Home in Burien, WA"
- "Adult Family Home in Des Moines, WA"
- "RN-owned Adult Family Home care"
- "Small 6-resident residential care"
- "Washington AFH care"
- "Magnolia Senior Loving Care"
- "Magnolia Senior Care of Highline"

### Lead with local identity

The Magnolia website should make location obvious in the first screen:

- Burien, WA
- Des Moines, WA
- Highline
- South King County

This helps families and Google understand that this Magnolia is not the multi-state assisted living brand.

### Lead with AFH model

Large assisted living and memory care brands often talk about communities, campuses, amenities, and corporate care teams.

Magnolia should talk about:

- A real home.
- Up to 6 residents.
- Personalized routines.
- Direct communication.
- RN-owned oversight.
- Family-style care.
- Easier access to the people managing the home.

### Use the full location names in SEO

The location pages should consistently use the real business names:

- Magnolia Senior Loving Care LLC
- Magnolia Senior Care of Highline LLC

This reduces confusion with other Magnolia-branded care businesses.

### Keep the domain Washington-specific

`magnoliacare.com` is shorter, but it is generic and expensive. It could fit many healthcare categories, not just Adult Family Homes.

`magnoliaseniorcarewa.com` is clearer for differentiation because it keeps the Magnolia Senior Care brand while signaling Washington, which separates this site from the Mississippi nursing home and the multi-state assisted living / memory care brand.

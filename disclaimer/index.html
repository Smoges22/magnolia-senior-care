Educational Disclaimer

Magnolia's Resource Center is designed to help families feel more informed, not to replace professional guidance for personal care, medical, legal, or financial decisions.

Last Updated: May 21, 2026

Please remember

For emergencies or urgent medical symptoms, call 911 or local emergency services. Do not rely on website content or website forms for emergency help.

Contact Magnolia for non-emergency questions

Educational content only

Magnolia Senior Care's Resource Center articles, service pages, location pages, and website materials are for general education. They are intended to help families understand questions, options, and next steps in senior care planning.

Not medical advice

Website content does not diagnose conditions, recommend treatment, replace a physician's advice, or provide emergency guidance. Families should consult physicians, nurses, pharmacists, therapists, hospice teams, or other qualified healthcare professionals for care-specific guidance.

Not legal or financial advice

Content about Medicaid, private pay, spend-down, long-term care planning, powers of attorney, or payment options is educational only. Medicaid rules and eligibility can change. Families should confirm details with Washington DSHS, HCS, elder law professionals, financial advisors, or other qualified professionals.

No guarantee of placement or payment

Magnolia cannot guarantee Medicaid approval, authorization, payment, placement, room availability, or a specific care arrangement through website content. Families should contact Magnolia directly to discuss current availability, care fit, assessment level, service requirements, and private-pay rates.

Hospice and end-of-life topics

Hospice and end-of-life Resource Center content is intended to support family understanding and communication. Families should work directly with hospice providers, physicians, and qualified care professionals for individual plans of care.

Emergency situations

If someone may be in immediate danger, experiencing a medical emergency, or needing urgent help, call 911 or local emergency services. Website forms and email are not emergency communication tools.
